## Quick Start

- browser extension: arxiv-utils, easyScholar
- manage literature: 在python中安装[autoliterature](https://github.com/WilmerWang/autoLiterature) or [a2b](https://github.com/kevinhongzl/a2b)

## 文献管理及阅读

> 【我是如何快速阅读和整理文献】<https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1nA41157y4?vd_source=93bb338120537438ee9180881deab9c1>

1. Filtrate: 

   - 在[Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/)中搜索文献，看摘要筛选一篇

     > - Semantic Scholar：**论文页信息结构化**、有 **TLDR**、Related papers、作者追踪、推荐等（更适合“快速筛选+沿图谱找文献”）
     > - Google Scholar：全但噪声大，有论文的各种版本

   - 在浏览器中下载easyScholar插件方便获取具体的期刊信息

   - 使用[Litmaps](https://app.litmaps.com/)根据引用查找文献

2. Download: use the autoliterature or a2b to download the paper info

   - To get full info: you can use [openalex](https://openalex.org/)

3. Read: several ways, you can check on the sidebar of ArXiv of Extra Services

   - download the paper to the local

   - read online: arxiv-utils支持用html渲染pdf论文。eg:

     对于论文链接`https://arxiv.org/abs/1512.03385`，就只需要替换其中的`arxiv`为`ar5iv`即可， `https://ar5iv.org/abs/1512.03385`然后打开就自动跳转了。 In this way, you can use the translate tools.

   - read with AI https://www.alphaxiv.org/

   - blog links

4. Make notes: 

   - I recongnise my notes about papers in the following way - Takeaway - Prior - Core Mechanism - Pipeline - Pros - Cons
   - 如果文献多了还可以使用mermaid建立框图

5. Discover relevant new papers: use the tools under the arxiv page

## Subscribe

不能仅依赖搜索，arXiv + OpenReview

- **arXiv**：按 category（比如 cs.LG / cs.CV / cs.CL / stat.ML）订阅

- **OpenReview**：会议（ICLR/NeurIPS/ICML）在审/录用论文经常更早出现讨论点
